Tournament Information

Welcome to the Brisbane Buzzwoles Sunday Tournaments page! This is the home for all tournaments hosted by the Brisbane Buzzwoles. We are based in the city of Brisbane in Queensland, Australia.

All of our tournaments will be held in the best-of-three format, in the most recent official Pokemon VGC format unless otherwise specified.
