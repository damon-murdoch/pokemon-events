Tournament Information

Welcome to the Brisbane Buzzwoles Sunday Tournaments page! This page is the home for all competitive Pokemon Video Game Championships tournaments hosted by us in Queensland, Australia. For the time being, we are unable to host officially sanctioned Play! Pokemon events however we are still able to host unofficial local tournaments for fun!

All of our tournaments will be held in the best-of-three format, in the most recent official Pokemon VGC format unless otherwise specified. These tournaments are mostly held just for fun and practise, however prizes may be offered for winners or random participants on the day. However, season points will be awarded to all competitors depending on placement and these will remain until the end of the season. The points breakdown can be seen below.

Definitions

Please feel free to reach out if you have any other questions!

  • Best of Three

    In a best-of-three tournament, players play a maximum of three games, or until one player has won two games. As opposed to a best of one tournament, where only one match is played each round to decide the winner.

  • Swiss Rounds

    In the swiss rounds of a tournament, you are paired up against a random opponent with the same (or as close as possible) win-loss record as you. You are not eliminated from the tournament if you lose multiple times, and can keep playing until the end of the tournament regardless of your win-loss record.

  • Top Cut

    For a tournament that has a top cut, the top-x players in the tournament (usually the top 4 or the top 8, depending on player count) make it into the top cut bracket. Those players then enter into a single elimination (one loss and you are out) bracket until a winner is decided.
